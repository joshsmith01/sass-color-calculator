Web Developers who want to take one color and convert it to another color can use SASS. If both colors are already known then the only thing left to do is to find the SCSS functions that bind the two colors together.

Sass Color Calculator finds that relationship. If your designer hands you an approved design and you want to use the most maintainable techniques to produce your product then you'll likely use Sass' built-in color functions.
